# 🛠️ Commands Reference

AgenGit is operated entirely through the `agit` command-line interface. Whether you're setting up a new repository, inspecting what your AI agent just did, or cleaning up your local store, there's a command for the job.

Explore the command categories below to learn more:

- **[Setup & Configuration](/docs/commands/setup)**: Start and stop hook integrations and shell ergonomics (`init`, `uninstall`, `version`, `completion`).
- **[Inspection & History](/docs/commands/inspection)**: Inspect sessions, steps, and diffs (`status`, `timeline`, `sessions`, `log`, `show`, `diff`, `between`, `cat`).
- **[Search & Analysis](/docs/commands/search-and-analysis)**: Retrieve and analyze prior work (`recall`, `grep`, `blame`, `eval`, `stats`).
- **[Maintenance & Health](/docs/commands/maintenance-and-health)**: Keep stores healthy and safe (`doctor`, `fsck`, `gc`, `privacy scan`, `reindex`).
- **[Remote & Sharing](/docs/commands/remote-and-sharing)**: Move captured history between environments (`push`, `pull`, `export`, `import`).
- **[Advanced Topics](/docs/commands/advanced)**: Experimental and live-monitoring workflows (`observe`, `watch`, `restore`).
