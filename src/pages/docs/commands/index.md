# 🛠️ Commands Reference

AgenGit is operated entirely through the `agit` command-line interface. Whether you're setting up a new repository, inspecting what your AI agent just did, or cleaning up your local store, there's a command for the job.

Explore the command categories below to learn more:

- **[Setup & Configuration](/docs/commands/setup)**: Commands to initialize repositories, configure agents, and manage integrations (`init`, `install`, `config`).
- **[Inspection & History](/docs/commands/inspection)**: Commands to view the history and status of your agent's work (`log`, `show`, `status`, `timeline`).
- **[Search & Analysis](/docs/commands/search-and-analysis)**: Commands to query your code and attribute lines to specific agents (`blame`, `grep`, `stats`).
- **[Maintenance & Health](/docs/commands/maintenance-and-health)**: Commands to maintain the integrity of your store and ensure things are running smoothly (`doctor`, `gc`, `fsck`, `privacy scan`, `reindex`).
- **[Remote & Sharing](/docs/commands/remote-and-sharing)**: Commands to export your session data or move it around safely (`export`, `import`).
- **[Advanced Topics](/docs/commands/advanced)**: Deep dives into the underlying architecture and manual object manipulation (`cat-file`, `hash-object`).
